BAH Calculator FAQ

What does this BAH calculator estimate?

It estimates projected monthly BAH, Base Pay, BAS, and total monthly military compensation using the selected duty location, grade, and years in service.

Does BAH change by location and dependents?

Yes. BAH can vary significantly by housing market and whether the member has dependents.

Can this feed into a housing affordability tool?

Yes. This calculator is designed to act as a clean first step before moving into a deeper mortgage and affordability workflow inside PCSUnited.
