About the PCS Move Calculator

PCSUnited’s PCS Move Calculator helps military users estimate their move-related cash flow before leaving one duty location for the next. It combines MALT, authorized travel days, estimated expenses, and available official entitlement data into one clean readiness-style view.

This tool is designed as the move-money companion to PCS Snapshot, BAH Calculator, and PCSUnited’s base intelligence workflow. Use it to preview whether your move may create a cash surplus, stay neutral, or require additional planning.

PCS Move Calculator FAQ

What does this PCS calculator estimate?

It estimates MALT, authorized PCS travel days, estimated move expenses, known entitlements, and your estimated net move position.

Why does DLA show as pending?

DLA requires the official rate table by grade and dependency status. PCSUnited’s engine is already structured for it, but the table should only be loaded from official source data.

Should I use official mileage?

Yes. For best results, enter the official PCS distance from your orders, finance office, or transportation office. Estimated map mileage may differ from official authorized mileage.

Current beta estimate includes MALT, authorized travel days, standard CONUS PCS per diem, and official HHG weight allowance checks. DLA will activate after the official DTMO table is loaded.
