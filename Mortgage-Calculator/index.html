About the PCSUnited Financial Health Snapshot

PCSUnited’s Financial Health Snapshot helps military users estimate mortgage payment pressure before moving into a deeper financial dashboard. It reads Financial Intake data when available, estimates monthly mortgage cost, and optionally uses TheWing.ai to calculate military compensation.

The core output is simple: total income minus mortgage payment and monthly obligations equals residual income. That residual margin becomes a lightweight financial health signal designed for fast PCS and home-buying decisions.

Financial Health Snapshot FAQ

What does this dashboard calculate?

It estimates your all-in mortgage payment, total monthly income, residual income, and residual income percentage after monthly obligations.

How is the financial health score calculated?

The snapshot uses residual margin: residual income divided by total monthly income. Higher residual margin generally means more breathing room after the mortgage and obligations.

Does this replace a lender approval?

No. This is a planning estimate, not underwriting, lending advice, or a formal loan approval decision.
