Retirement & VA Calculator FAQ

What does this calculator estimate?

It estimates projected monthly military retirement pay, VA disability compensation, and combined monthly income using the selected retirement and disability inputs.

Does retirement pay change by retirement system?

Yes. Retirement pay can vary based on whether the member falls under High-3 or the Blended Retirement System, in addition to rank and years of service.

Can this support broader financial planning?

Yes. This calculator is designed to act as a clean first step before moving into deeper income planning, housing, and retirement-readiness workflows inside PCSUnited.
